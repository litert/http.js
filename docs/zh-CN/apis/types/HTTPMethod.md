# 类型 HTTPMethod

该类型用于约束可以使用的 HTTP 方法名称。

## 定义

```ts
type HTTPMethod = "GET" | "POST" | "PUT" | "TRACE" |
"DELETE" | "OPTIONS" | "HEAD" | "PATCH" |
"COPY" | "LOCK" | "UNLOCK" | "MOVE" |
"MKCOL" | "PROPFIND" | "PROPPATCH" | "REPORT" |
"MKACTIVITY" | "CHECKOUT" | "MERGE" | "M-SEARCH" |
"NOTIFY" | "SUBSCRIBE" | "UNSUBSCRIBE";
```
